Encomienda System

The Encomienda system was a labor system made by the Spanish Empire. This was a coerced labor system that granted endcomenderos tracks of land and the Natives who inhanited the land in order to make profit on goods like gold and silver. Encomenderos were supposed to provide protection and educate the Natives and turn them Christian. Gradually, Natives were replaced with African labor because the Natives kept dying. This Encomienda system also led to the development of a very complex caste system.

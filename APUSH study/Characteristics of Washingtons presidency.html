Characteristics of Washington’s presidency

Known as the “precedent president” because he had to pave the path for future American leaders. He tried to have neutrality with all foreign powers rather than alliances. He opposed political powers and left after 2 terms. He did support the national bank and Hamilton’s financial plan. He had a cabinet of 4 qualified men. He got the port of New Orleans and shows the presidential strength after the Whiskey Rebellion.

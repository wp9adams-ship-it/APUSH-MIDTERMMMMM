What was Metacom's War (King Philip's War)?

Metacom's war was a war between New England settlers and Native American tribes led by Chief Metacom. It was extremely devastating with 10% of New Englanders perishing in the conflict. Ultimately, it resulted in a Native defeat and English cobtrol over the region was strengthened as Natives had their power reduced.
